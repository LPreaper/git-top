Chicken Burrito

Description:

This Quick and easy recipe is great for a post workout meal.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of Escalope.
  • 2 tomatoes.
  • 2 onions.
  • 2 green peppers.

Steps :

  1. Place escalope in a pot. Season with salt and black pepper and cover it with natural yogurt.
  2. Cut the vegetables into tiny pieces.
  3. Start Frying The cut tomatoes.
  4. Add the onion to the pan and fry until it looks like caramel
  5. DFinally add the peppers.
  6. Fry the escalope.
  7. Put the mixture into bread.
  8. Enjoy your sandwich
