Chicken Burrito

Description:

This Recipe gives you a protein-rich breakfast that is both filling and easy to make.

The best thing about this dish is the fact that it is so simple to make. You can prepare it in 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • chocolate powder
  • honey

Steps :

  1. Place Milk in pot and have it start warming up.
  2. Add A teaspoon of powder to the bowl
  3. Add honey
  4. Take the now warm milk out of the pot and place it in the bowl.
  5. Mix it well.
  6. Enjoy!
